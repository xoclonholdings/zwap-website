Account & Data Request

ZWAP! Account Deletion

Use this page to request deletion of your ZWAP! account and associated data. Requests are reviewed and processed within 7 days.

Submit a deletion request

Complete the form below using the email address connected to your ZWAP! account.

You may also email your request directly to admin@zwap.online with the subject “Delete My Account.”

What data will be deleted?

When your request is verified, ZWAP! will delete your account and associated user data.

  • Email address
  • Account information
  • Activity data, including steps, gameplay, and learning progress
  • zPts balance and progression data
  • Streaks, badges, and app progress connected to the account

What may be retained?

Minimal records may be retained only where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute handling, or abuse prevention.

Retained records are limited to what is necessary for those purposes and are not used for normal account functionality after deletion.

Processing timeframe

Account and associated data deletion requests are processed within 7 days after the request is received and verified.

  1. Submit the form above or email admin@zwap.online.
  2. Use the email address connected to your ZWAP! account.
  3. ZWAP! will process the request within 7 days.
