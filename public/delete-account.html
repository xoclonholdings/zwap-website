ZWAP! Account Deletion

To request deletion of your ZWAP! account and associated data:

1. Send an email to:

admin@zwap.online

2. Use subject: Delete My Account

3. Include your account email address

Your account and associated data will be deleted within 7 days.

Data deleted:

Account info, activity data, zPts, progress

Data retained (if required):

Minimal records for security and fraud prevention
